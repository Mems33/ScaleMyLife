# 🏛️ Areas

An **area** is a role or responsibility with a *standard to maintain* and *no finish line*: health, finances, being a good friend. Projects end; areas endure. Most notes you keep long-term hang off an area.

Each area note (from [[07 - Templates/Area|the Area template]]) states **why it matters**, the **standard** you hold yourself to, current **habits/routines**, and links to its active projects and key resources. Review each area monthly — that's where drift is caught: no activity in an area for a month is a signal, not a coincidence.

## ScaleMyLife tie-in

These areas should mirror your **life areas / skills** in ScaleMyLife. The app levels them up as you do tagged work; these notes hold the *why* and the *how* behind each one. When the app's stats show an area starving, the area note tells you what "back on track" means.
