# 🔁 Workflows

The system runs on three loops. Miss one occasionally — fine. Miss all of them for two weeks and the vault starts lying to you.

## Daily (5–10 min)

**Morning (2 min):**
1. `Ctrl/Cmd+P → "Daily note"` — today's note from the template.
2. Write the top-3 intentions. The #1 should match your #1 in ScaleMyLife.

**During the day (0 min):**
- Capture into the daily note's Log or as new inbox notes. Don't organise. Don't interrupt work to file.

**Evening (5 min):**
1. Journal section: what happened, mood, gratitude, tomorrow's #1.
2. Tick off habits & log the day in ScaleMyLife (journal there too — one line is enough; this vault holds the long version).
3. Quick inbox skim: anything urgent → tomorrow's intentions.

## Weekly (30 min) — from [[07 - Templates/Weekly Review|the Weekly Review template]]

Clear (inbox → zero, loose tasks swept) → Review (projects have next actions, dead ones archived, ScaleMyLife Week-in-Review read) → Plan (top 3 outcomes, weekly boss picked, calendar checked).

Create it as a note named `Weekly Review YYYY-WW` in `01 - Daily` — insert the template with `Ctrl/Cmd+P → "Templates: Insert template"`.

## Monthly (1 h) — from [[07 - Templates/Monthly Review|the Monthly Review template]]

Areas audited against their standards, project portfolio pruned, money check-in, three permanent notes reread and relinked, the big three questions answered.

## When you read/watch something good

1. Create a note from [[07 - Templates/Source|the Source template]] in `04 - Resources`.
2. Capture ideas *in your own words* while reading; quotes only for the truly great lines.
3. After finishing, write the summary from memory.
4. Any idea that changed your mind → its own [[07 - Templates/Zettel|permanent note]], linked both ways.

## When a new commitment appears

Gets a deadline and an end? → [[07 - Templates/Project|Project note]] + main quest in ScaleMyLife. Open-ended? → attach to an [[03 - Areas/Areas|Area]] as a habit or routine.
