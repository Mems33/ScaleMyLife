# 🎮 ScaleMyLife + Second Brain

Two systems, one life. They deliberately don't overlap:

| | **ScaleMyLife** (the app) | **This vault** (the brain) |
|---|---|---|
| Job | Motivation & execution | Memory & thinking |
| Unit | XP, coins, streaks, quests | Notes, links, ideas |
| Question it answers | *"Did I do the work?"* | *"What do I know, and what should the work be?"* |
| Time horizon | Today, this week | Months, years |

## The handshake points

- **Main quest ↔ Project note.** Every main quest in the app gets a project note here (and vice-versa). App tracks steps/XP/focus-time; the note holds scope, research, decisions.
- **Life areas ↔ Area notes.** Same names on both sides. The app levels them; [[03 - Areas/Areas|the notes]] define their standards and hold their knowledge.
- **Journal ↔ Journal.** One line in the app (it feeds the mood/insight stats); the full reflection lives in the daily note here.
- **Weekly boss ↔ Weekly review.** Pick the boss *during* the [[07 - Templates/Weekly Review|weekly review]], informed by the app's Week-in-Review stats.
- **Habits live in the app** (they pay XP and show chains). Area notes only describe the *plan* behind them.

## The rule of thumb

**Doing → app. Thinking → vault.** If you're logging effort, tracking a streak, or need a push — that's ScaleMyLife. If you're capturing an idea, making a decision, or storing something future-you needs — that's here.
