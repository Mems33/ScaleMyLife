# 📥 Inbox

Everything you capture lands here first — new notes default to this folder, so capturing is one hotkey (`Ctrl/Cmd+N`), type, done. **Don't organise while capturing.** Deciding where something goes is a different job, done later with fresh eyes.

## Capture rules

- If it takes under 10 seconds, capture it. A title alone is fine.
- One thought per note beats one giant "misc" note.
- On mobile, use Obsidian mobile's quick-capture or email yourself and transfer.

## Processing (daily, ~5 min — details in [[90 - Meta/Workflows|Workflows]])

For each note ask **"where will I use this?"**

1. **Actionable now?** → task in today's [[07 - Templates/Daily Note|daily note]] or quest in ScaleMyLife.
2. **Part of a short-term effort?** → move to `02 - Projects` (link it from the project note).
3. **Part of an ongoing responsibility?** → move to `03 - Areas`.
4. **Interesting reference?** → move to `04 - Resources`.
5. **A genuine idea of my own?** → rewrite as a permanent note in `05 - Zettelkasten`.
6. **None of the above?** → delete, or `06 - Archive` if you can't bear to.

The inbox is a **corridor, not a room**. If it holds more than ~20 notes, your reviews are slipping.
