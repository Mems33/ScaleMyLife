# 🎯 Projects

A **project** is anything with a *goal* and an *end*: "Pass the June exam", "Ship the leaderboard feature", "Plan the Italy trip". If it can't fail and never finishes, it's an [[03 - Areas/Areas|Area]], not a project.

## Rules of thumb

- Every project gets **one note** created from [[07 - Templates/Project|the Project template]] — outcome, deadline, next actions, and links to everything related.
- Keep the list honest: **3–7 active projects**. More than that and nothing moves.
- A project with no *next action* is stalled — surface it in the [[07 - Templates/Weekly Review|weekly review]].
- Done or dead → move the note to `06 - Archive`. The links keep working.

## ScaleMyLife tie-in

A project here should usually exist as a **main quest** in ScaleMyLife. The app tracks the doing (steps, XP, focus time banked on the quest); the note here holds the thinking (scope, decisions, research, links). One is the scoreboard, the other is the war room.
