# 🗄️ Archive

Finished projects, retired areas, resources that stopped being interesting. **Archive instead of deleting** — links keep working, search still finds it, and cold storage costs nothing.

Two habits keep this painless:

- When a project ends (shipped or abandoned), move its note here *during the weekly review* and add one line at the top: what happened and why.
- Never reorganise the archive. It's write-only. If something becomes relevant again, move it back out — that's the only read.
