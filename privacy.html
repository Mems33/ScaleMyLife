🏆 ScaleMyLife - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

ScaleMyLife is a personal habit and productivity game. The short version: your data is yours, it stays on your device unless you opt into cloud sync, and we run no ads, no analytics and no tracking of any kind.

What the app stores, and where

On your device. Everything you enter (quests, habits, journal entries, mood and sleep logs, focus sessions, character customization, settings) is saved in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device unless you turn on cloud sync.

Cloud sync (optional). If you create an account, your email address, a password hash and a copy of your game save are stored in our database (hosted by Supabase) so your progress follows you across devices. Your save is protected by row-level security: only your signed-in account can read it. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; we never see or store them in plain text.

Leaderboard and friends (optional). Joining the leaderboard or enabling friends shares exactly six fields: your hero name, avatar, level, rank, weekly XP and best streak. Never your quests, habits, journal or any other content. Leaving the leaderboard deletes that row entirely.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no ad networks.
  • No analytics or telemetry - we cannot see how you use the app.
  • No tracking cookies. The only browser storage used is what the game itself needs to function.
  • No selling, renting or sharing of data with anyone.

Third-party services

  • Supabase - database and authentication, only if you enable cloud sync.
  • Google Fonts - loads the app's typefaces.
  • YouTube (privacy-enhanced mode) - only if you choose focus music, the player is embedded from youtube-nocookie.com and only after you pick a station.

Your rights and your data

  • Export - download your full save any time as JSON (⚙️ Settings → Export save).
  • Delete local data - ⚙️ Settings → Reset everything.
  • Leave the leaderboard - removes your public row immediately.
  • Delete your account and cloud data - open an issue at github.com/Mems33/ScaleMyLife/issues and it will be removed.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences and collects no personal information beyond the optional account email described above.

Changes
